In an age where humor, wit, and cultural references collide in the digital realm, memes have emerged as the universal language of the internet. They entertain, enlighten, and connect people across the globe, transcending boundaries and generations. Welcome to Memedom, your one-stop destination for all things meme-related. Dive into a world where laughter knows no limits, and wit reigns supreme as we explore the fascinating universe of internet humor and creativity.

The term "meme" describes an idea, behavior, style, or usage that spreads from person to person within a culture, originally coined by Richard Dawkins.
